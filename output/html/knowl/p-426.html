This is true for all \(n \in \mathbb{Z}\) however we will only prove it for positive integers. This can be proved without induction, since at least one of \(n\) and \((n + 1)\) is even (if \(n\) is even, then \((n + 1)\) is odd, and if \(n\) is odd, then \((n + 1)\) is even).
